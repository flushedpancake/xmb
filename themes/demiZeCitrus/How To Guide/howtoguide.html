Welcome to the how to guide on installing themes to your XMB Software.

Step 1: Go to the administration panel and clock on the link that says "Themes". (See demo below)

Step 2: Now that you have done Step 1 you should scroll to the bottom of the themes page. (See demo below)

Step 3: Now click the "Browse..." button and locate the theme file located in the "Import File" folder then click the "Import Theme into XMB" button. Your theme is now installed.

Step 4: Now open your preferred ftp software and locate the theme folder for the theme you imported and upload it to your forum "images" folder located in your forum directory.

 

That's all. Enjoy!
