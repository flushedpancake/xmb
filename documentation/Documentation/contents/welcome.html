Welcome to XMB 1.9.8 Engage! With 1.9.8 Engage you can expect several new features, a more secure environment due to validation routines, as well as fixes to bugs and quirks that were previously reported. 1.9.8 Engage is the succesor to 1.9.7 Nexus but is not based on 1.9.7's codebase. To create 1.9.8 Engage, the codebase was rolled back to John Brigg's service pack 1 of 1.9.5.

The navigation bar above will direct you through this pre-installation documentation. If you are in need of further help, direct your browser to the support page which is linked above on the navigation bar.
